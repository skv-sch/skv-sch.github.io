SKV Prefects

Empowering student leaders who embody excellence, discipline, and service in our school community

Student Leadership Excellence

At Sri Kalyanathissa Vidyalaya, our prefects are carefully selected student leaders who demonstrate outstanding character, academic excellence, and commitment to serving their peers. They play a vital role in maintaining school discipline, fostering a positive learning environment, and upholding the Buddhist values that form the foundation of our institution.

Our prefectship program, established alongside the school in 1974, has produced countless leaders who continue to serve their communities with honor and distinction.
