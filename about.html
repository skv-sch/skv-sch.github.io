About Our School

Discover the rich heritage and values that make Sri Kalyanathissa Vidyalaya a beacon of excellence in education since 1974.

Our Story

Sri Kalyanathissa Vidyalaya, located in Batapola, Galle District, was established in 1974 with a vision to blend traditional Sri Lankan values with modern education.

Serving over 1,385 students from Grades 1 to 11, the school focuses on holistic development through a balanced curriculum of academics, arts, and sports, deeply rooted in Buddhist principles and global educational standards.

From a modest beginning, the school has grown into a premier educational institution in the Southern Province. Our rich legacy of academic excellence, cultural pride, and community service continues to inspire future generations of students.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To nurture curious minds and create responsible citizens who will lead Sri Lanka into the future. We are committed to providing holistic education that combines academic excellence with character development, cultural values, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

Education for a brighter future, fostering innovation and cultural values in every student. We envision a world where our graduates become leaders, innovators, and compassionate individuals who contribute positively to society.

Core Values

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in academics, character, and personal development.

Integrity

Building honest, ethical, and responsible individuals who uphold moral principles.

Community

Fostering a sense of belonging, cooperation, and service to our local and global community.

Innovation

Encouraging creativity, critical thinking, and adaptation to modern challenges.

Tradition

Preserving and celebrating Sri Lankan Buddhist culture and heritage in our modern world.

Global Perspective

Preparing students to thrive in an interconnected world while staying rooted in local values.

Our Journey

1974

Foundation

Sri Kalyanathissa Vidyalaya was established with a vision to provide quality education rooted in Buddhist principles and Sri Lankan cultural values.

1980s

Growth & Development

The school expanded its facilities and curriculum, introducing modern teaching methods while maintaining traditional values.

1990s

Academic Excellence

Achieved recognition for outstanding academic performance and began producing top performers in national examinations.

2000s

Modernization

Introduced computer education, modern laboratories, and enhanced sports facilities to meet contemporary educational needs.

2010s

Digital Integration

Embraced digital learning technologies and expanded extracurricular programs to provide holistic education.

Today

Continuing Legacy

Now serving over 1,385 students, we continue to evolve while staying true to our founding principles of excellence and values-based education.
