About Our School

Founded in 1974, our school has been a beacon of knowledge and culture in Sri Lanka. We combine traditional values with modern education techniques to nurture well-rounded individuals.

Our Mission

To nurture curious minds and create responsible citizens who will lead Sri Lanka into the future.

Our Vision

Education for a brighter future, fostering innovation and cultural values in every student.
